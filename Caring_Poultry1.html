  • ‚úîÔ∏è Ensure a balanced diet: Commercial feeds are typically balanced; supplements should be given carefully

ü•£ Common Feed Types
Starter Feed
High protein (20-24%) for chicks (0-8 weeks) to support rapid growth.
Grower Feed
Moderate protein (16-18%) for young pullets/broilers (8-18 weeks) before laying/finishing.
Layer Feed
Balanced protein (16-18%) with added calcium for laying hens (18+ weeks) for strong eggshells.
Broiler Finisher
Specific blend for meat birds in their final weeks to promote muscle gain and fat deposition.
